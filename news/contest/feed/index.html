]]> The Rupaya Twitter Contest

Central to Rupaya is our community. That’s why our most recent governance marketing proposal included giveaways to reward people that actively participate and engage on a regular basis.

To that end, we’re doing a little social media contest for the next week. Be sure to read carefully to ensure that you are in the running for rewards!

RULES:

  • Each day, up to 8 (eight) Engagement Awards of 250 RUPX. Engagement Awards will be given out to the tweets that receive the most engagement (RTs, likes and comments)
  • Each day, up to 2 (two) Rupayan Awards of 250 RUPX. Rupayan Awards will be given out to the tweets deemed as being the best written (could be the most clever, funny, community driven).
  • Follow the guidelines below for posting on Twitter. Adding your own content is highly encouraged, especially to win a Rupayan Award!
  • Hashtags may include both the $ and # varieties (for instance $RUPX does not need anything added to it)
  • Content in shown in brackets [] must be replaced with the correct content!
